Bio

Pierce the Veil is an American rock band from San Diego, California. This band was founded by two brothers, Vic and Mike Fuentes (see below) in 2006. Over the course of PTV's career, they have released 5 albums and a total of 60 songs. To see what the band is up to, you can go to their website. If you want to support Pierce the Veil, click here to visit their merch page.

The band members are:

  • Victor "Vic" Fuentes
    • Lead Singer/Guitar
    • Born: February 10, 1983 (age 36)
  • Michael Christopher "Mike" Fuentes
    • Drums
    • Born: December 14, 1984 (age 34)
  • Jaime Preciado
    • Bass Guitar
    • Born: May 17, 1986 (age 32)
  • Tony Perry
    • Lead Guitar
    • Born: February 25, 1986 (age 33)
